## üß† **Section 5 ‚Äì Remember This**

* Hindi script is called **Devanagari**.
* Write **left to right** with a top line joining letters.
* **13 vowels** and **33 consonants** make up the alphabet.
* Hindi is **phonetic** ‚Äî you write what you speak.
